About Me

I, Akshay Rajendra Kedar , am a passionate and skilled second-year diploma student from Government Polytechnic Pune, proficient in front-end and back-end development. Beyond mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I've advanced my expertise with GSAP for animations, and built scalable applications using Node.js, Express, and MongoDB with Mongoose. I'm the creator of GuruKilli — an innovative AI-powered gamified learning platform — and have proven my technical prowess through successful hackathon participations and impactful collaborations with schools. I'm committed to revolutionizing education by blending creativity, technology, and real-world application.
